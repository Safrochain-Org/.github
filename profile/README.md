# Hi, we're Safrochain.

We are building Safrochain, a community first Layer 1 blockchain on the Cosmos SDK, made by and for builders across Africa and the wider world. Our goal is simple. Give people the tools to launch real products, real tokens, and real apps on a chain that is open, fast, and affordable.

## What we work on

Safrochain Node is the heart of the network. Around it we maintain modules for token issuance, fee sharing, contract automation, and smart contract hooks, plus the tooling that ties it all together. Take a look at our pinned repositories to see what we ship.

## What we believe

Builders come first. If a developer cannot understand a feature in ten minutes, that is a bug in our docs, not in the developer.

The chain belongs to its community. Decisions are made in the open, on GitHub and on chain, and we keep the conversation friendly to newcomers.

Africa deserves great infrastructure. Local teams should own the rails their products run on, not rent them from somewhere else.

Thanks for stopping by. If you ship something on Safrochain, tell us. We love seeing what people build.
