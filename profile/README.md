# Hi, we're Safrochain.

We are building Safrochain, a community first Layer 1 blockchain built on the Cosmos SDK, made by and for builders across Africa and the wider world. Our goal is simple. Give people the tools to launch real products, real tokens, and real applications on top of an open, fast, and affordable chain.

## What we work on

Safrochain Node is the heart of the network. Around it we maintain modules for token issuance, fee sharing, contract automation, smart contract hooks, and developer friendly tooling. Everything is open source and welcomes outside contributions.

If you are curious about what we ship, take a look at our pinned repositories below. You will find the chain itself, the SDK glue that wires it together, sample CosmWasm contracts, and the tools we use to run and operate the network.

## What we believe

Three ideas guide most of what we do.

First, builders come first. If a developer cannot understand a feature in under ten minutes, we treat that as a bug in our documentation, not in the developer.

Second, the chain belongs to its community. Decisions that shape Safrochain are discussed in the open, on GitHub and on chain through governance, and we work hard to keep those conversations approachable for newcomers.

Third, Africa deserves great infrastructure. Safrochain is grounded in the conviction that local teams should own the rails their products run on, rather than always renting them from somewhere else.

Thanks for stopping by. If you ship something on Safrochain, tell us. We love seeing what people build.
